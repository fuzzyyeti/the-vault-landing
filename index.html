Interest bearing derivatives

Welcome to the new DeFi frontier! Traditional Stake derivatives are value accruing. With The Vaults solution, you can now have interest bearing stake derivatives. Use your derivative in DeFi but claim the stake rewards separately!

VIDEO PRES

Using the latest token22 features, such as TransferHook, we have created a token derivative that has its related stake rewards separated from the derivative, and it is trackable across DeFi!

With DeFi rewards separated from the derivative, it is possible for DeFi users to manage their DeFi portfolio (and staked assets) in a different way compared to today.

Lets look at a couple of DeFi use case examples:

vSOL - a staked SOL derivative

Stake SOL for vSOL. vSOL bears interest that can be claimed at the vault after each epoch, or whenever it suits the user, no matter where the vSOL is used in DeFi.

More than a stake derivative!

Our solution is suitable for more avenues in DeFi than just staking. Expanding on what weve built one can create new types of stable coin derivatives or even play around with bond mechanics!

Delegate rewards to a separate account

Easily delegate stake rewards from the vTOKEN to a separate account. Our solution provides multiple avenues to take with leveraging DeFi for payments and economy sharing. Some examples of the way one could use DeFi/stake yield is:

- Pay for a subscription with the yield
- Donate yield to charity
- Donate yield to your local gaming streamer

Stake rewards as a payment method??

Imagine being able to pay for your Netflix subscription with yield from your DeFi stake positions, effectively saving money while paying for services.
